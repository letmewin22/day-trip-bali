Day trip to Nusa Penida by boat

This is not a tour — this is a real boat adventure!

Come with us on this amazing one of a kind day trip to Nusa Penida. It is a wonderful way to spend the day at sea with your friends and family.

We will depart from Bali and head to Nusa Penida where we will swim with Manta Rays, snorkel among colorful fish and coral reefs, and explore some amazing beaches and lagoons. You can enjoy a nice lunch on the island, and then go sightseeing as well.

Sit back, relax and enjoy your adventure, our crew will take care of the rest.
