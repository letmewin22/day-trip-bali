This is not a tour — this is a real boat adventure!

We offer you something special - In just one day you have the opportunity to cruise by private boat, swim with giant oceanic Manta Rays, snorkel in coral gardens with tropical fish and visit the most beautiful places in Nusa Penida island.

What will we do:

• Swim with giant oceanic Manta Rays
• Snorkeling in 3 different spots
• Lunch in nice restaurant with infinity pool and volcano view
• Visit Kelingking cliff during car tour
• Cruising beatiful lagoons
