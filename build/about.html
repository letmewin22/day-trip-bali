The beginning
of Day Trip Bali

We have been started our firsts tours in 2014, in that time not many people know about Nusa Penida island. It was amazing wild place, full of green tropical jungles, white send beaches without any people, no restaurants, no hotels, no traffic jams there.

The waters around was full of fish and corals, and it was the rare place where we can see the giant Oceanic Manta Rays in wild habitat.

For last years Nusa Penida become more popular for visitors form all over the world, but still has the amazing atmosphere of wild adventure there. And we share this atmosphere with you!
